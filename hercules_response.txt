### 1. Introduction and Program Context
Let's build a classic Push-Pull-Legs workout split focused on maximizing muscle hypertrophy and strength.

### 2. Volume (Sets per Muscle Group)
- **Chest:** 12 sets/week
- **Back:** 12 sets/week
- **Shoulders:** 9 sets/week
- **Legs (Quads/Hamstrings):** 12 sets/week
- **Arms (Biceps/Triceps):** 9 sets/week
- **Calves:** 6 sets/week

### 3. Workout Program

#### Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
- **Bench Press:** 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Double progression model
- **Overhead Press:** 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Double progression model
- **Incline Dumbbell Press:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Add weight when hitting top reps in all sets
- **Lateral Raises:** 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  - Intensity: 0-1 RIR
  - Rest: 1-1.5 minutes
  - Progression: Increase reps before adding weight
- **Tricep Pushdowns:** 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  - Intensity: 0-1 RIR
  - Rest: 1-1.5 minutes
  - Progression: Increase reps before adding weight

#### Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)
- **Pull-Ups:** 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Add reps or weight when possible
- **Barbell Rows:** 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Double progression model
- **Face Pulls:** 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 1-1.5 minutes
  - Progression: Increase reps before adding weight
- **Hammer Curls:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  - Intensity: 0-1 RIR
  - Rest: 1-1.5 minutes
  - Progression: Increase reps before adding weight

#### Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)
- **Squats:** 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 3-4 minutes
  - Progression: Double progression model
- **Romanian Deadlifts:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Add weight when hitting top reps
- **Leg Press:** 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  - Intensity: 1-2 RIR
  - Rest: 2-3 minutes
  - Progression: Increase reps before adding weight
- **Calf Raises:** 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  - Intensity: 0-1 RIR
  - Rest: 1-1.5 minutes
  - Progression: Increase reps before adding weight

#### Rest Days
- **Sunday and Thursday**: Rest days for optimal recovery.

Remember to start each workout with a proper warm-up, gradually increasing the intensity with warm-up weights before hitting your working sets. Enjoy your training journey!
